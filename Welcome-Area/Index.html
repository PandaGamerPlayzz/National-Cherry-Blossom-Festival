Welcome Area

 Welcome to DC's Cherry Blossom Festival, where you can appreciate the blossoms through outstanding exhibitions and activities! Create your own Pink Tie Dinner Party with some delicious cherry blossom-related recipes.

What's included in the Cherry Blossom Festival:

  • An Opening Ceremony you can watch
  • A Peak Blossom Calendar
  • The Petalpalooza with exhibitions and family-friendly hands-on activities
  • The DIY Kite Competition
  • The Porch Decorating Competition
  • An article on the History of the Cherry Blossom Festival
  • The Pink Tie Dinner Party with cherry blossom-related recipes to try
