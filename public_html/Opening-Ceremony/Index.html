Opening Ceremony

 Every year, the Opening Ceremony recognizes the cultural significance behind the gift of Cherry Blossoms in 1912 and celebrates the unrelenting friendship between Japan and the U.S. Enjoy one-of-a-kind performances by artists connected to the two countries!
